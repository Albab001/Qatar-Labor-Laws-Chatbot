# 🇶🇦 Qatar Labor Laws Assistant

An AI-powered chatbot that provides accurate answers about Qatar Labor Law through Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG).

## 🌟 Features

- **Accurate Information**: Answers based on official Qatar labor law documents
- **RAG-Powered**: Uses advanced retrieval techniques to find relevant information
- **User-Friendly**: Natural conversation interface with helpful guidance
- **Smart Query Handling**: Gracefully handles both valid questions and casual inputs
- **Real-Time Responses**: Fast answers through MCP integration with n8n

## 💡 What Can You Ask?

This assistant can help you with:

- 📅 Working hours and schedules (including Ramadan hours)
- 🏖️ Leave entitlements (annual, sick, maternity leave)
- 📝 Employment contracts and probation periods
- 🔚 Termination and notice periods
- 💰 Overtime and compensation
- ⚖️ Employee rights and employer obligations
- 📜 General Qatar labor law regulations

## 🎯 Example Questions

Try asking:
- "What are the standard working hours in Qatar?"
- "How many days of annual leave am I entitled to?"
- "What is the probation period in Qatar?"
- "Tell me about overtime pay regulations"
- "What is the notice period for termination?"
- "How many hours can I work during Ramadan?"

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: Gradio 5.12.0
- **Backend**: Model Context Protocol (MCP)
- **Workflow**: n8n automation
- **Architecture**: RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)
- **Data Source**: Official Qatar Labor Law documents

## 🚀 How It Works

1. **User Query**: You ask a question about Qatar labor law
2. **Query Processing**: The system validates and processes your query
3. **Document Retrieval**: RAG searches through official documents to find relevant information
4. **Answer Generation**: AI generates an accurate answer based on retrieved documents
5. **Response**: You receive a clear, informative answer

## 📖 About Qatar Labor Law

This chatbot is based on Qatar's official labor law regulations. The information provided is for general guidance. For legal advice or specific cases, please consult with a qualified legal professional or the Qatar Ministry of Labour.

## 🔒 Privacy & Data

- No personal data is stored
- Conversations are not saved
- Each session is independent
- All queries are processed securely
